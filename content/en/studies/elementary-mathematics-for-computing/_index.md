I'm reviewing and studying high school mathematics with a focus on practical applications in computing. The goal is to strengthen my mathematical foundation to take the next step in knowledge and prepare for future projects like the Computer Science course at [UBL - Universidade Brasileira Livre](https://ulivre.dev). Some topics are review, others are content I haven't mastered yet.

## About the curriculum

This project follows the [Elementary Mathematics for Computing](https://matematica.pgdinamica.com/) curriculum, an open source material created by Hallison Paz and Kizzy Terra from [Programação Dinâmica](https://pgdinamica.com/). The curriculum covers everything from logic and sets to trigonometry and probability, always connecting mathematics with real applications in computing. For more details about the curriculum, visit the [official project page](https://matematica.pgdinamica.com/).

## How it works

During the week I study the curriculum topics, take notes in Jupyter Notebooks and solve exercises. On Sundays, I compile everything into a post and record a video for my YouTube channel [@walissonaguirra](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLq6JUH4gQT2ZPSKnMagcLdrbssFH2ou6Q) documenting what I studied. The channel is my way of tracking progress and exchanging ideas with others who are also studying.

As I progress through the studies, each topic below gets a link to the post with the notes and corresponding video.
